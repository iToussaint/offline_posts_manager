## App Demonstration

In the video below, I demonstrate the full CRUD lifecycle of the application. 

### Key Highlights:
- **Persistence Check**: I perform a **Hot Restart** after creating a post to demonstrate that the data is stored locally in **SQLite** and persists across app sessions.
- **CRUD Implementation**:
  - **Create**: Adding posts.
  - **Read**: Viewing the list and post details.
  - **Update**: Modifying existing post data.
  - **Delete**: Removing posts from the database.
